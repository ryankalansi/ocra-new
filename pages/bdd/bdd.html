Unsung Heroes:
Beyond the Blueprints 1.0

A Collaborative Installation for Bintaro Design District

Rootslab, K-Thengono Design Studio, Digital Nativ, Grey Audio Lab, Ocra, and Riyan Berlian present “Unsung Heroes: Beyond the Blueprints 1.0” at Bintaro Design District 2024. Inspired by the grass root culture of “Gotong Royong” and the craftsmanship value of Indonesian “Tukang”, the installation combines analog techniques with technology, featuring a pavilion modeled after the “Bedeng Tukang.” It highlights sound as a unifying force and reuses materials to create an interactive, sensory experience that honors the artistry of human touch.

This installation explores the intersection of community, labor, and technology. By reimagining the workshop as a sensory playground, it invites visitors to engage with architecture in new ways and pays tribute to the unsung workers behind every built structure, reconnecting with the analog roots of community-building and labor.
